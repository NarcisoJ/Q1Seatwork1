• Characters of Noli Me Tangere
    • Crisostomo Ibarra Y Magsalin
      The protagonist or bida in the story Noli Me Tangere. This character is used to show the Philippines before and is symbolising Jose Rizal. Crisostomo was an honest and idealistic man. He studied in Europe in year 1874.
    • Padre Damaso
      The antagonist or kontrabida in the story. This character shows how arrogant and selfish people were in their time. Ibarra's competitor. Damaso was a Spanish Friar and priest, he was tagged opinionated and superior.
    • Don Rafael Ibarra
      The father of Crisostomo Ibarra. He was known as the richest in the city, he was generous and benevolent. He was labeled as Erehe at Pilibustero in Chapter 4.
    • Kapitan Tiago
      The father of Maria Clara and a business man in San Diego. He is a rich native Filipino and is known for his hospitality to fellow people.
    • Maria Clara
      The childhood sweetheart and fiance of Ibarra. She was raised by Kapitan Tiago and Tia Isabel because her Mom died a couple of weeks after giving birth.
